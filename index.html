River Safari

Asia’s first and only river-themed wildlife park!

Occupying 12 hectares and developed at S$160 million, the park is home to 400 plant species and over 7,500 aquatic and terrestrial animals
representing close to 240 species, including one of the world’s largest collections of freshwater animals.



Activities in River Safari

Exciting activities await you at River Safari!

Get ready for an unforgettable journey into the mysterious wilderness through the Amazon River Quest, River Discovery Tour and many more!


Zones to Explore


Amazon Flooded Forest

This exhibit features our giant river otters that are on display for the first time in Asia! Come cheek to cheek with Chico and Carla through the viewing tunnel or at the sankbank on the upper level when they're enjoying their afternoon siesta! Keep an eye out for their animated conversations through nine different calls!

Dont miss out on our other residents of the Amazon Rainforest such as Manatee, Arapaima, Electric eel, Oscar fish and many others!


Giant Panda Forest

This exhibit spans 1,500 square metres, the largest panda exhibit built in Southeast Asia!
Get a peek into the natural habitat of the Giant Panda and the Red Panda, where waterfall with shallow pools and climbing structures are included to recreate their actual living environment.


Ganges River

The Ganges River features an array of South Asian species like the fish-eating Indian gharial, frog-faced softshell turtle and the intriguing goonch catfish!


Squirrel Monkey Forest

The most abundant monkey species of the Amazon Rainforest, the squirrel monkey forms troops of as many as 300 in the wild!

Our monkeys transverse the Squirrel Monkey Forest with their "urine-washed" hands and feet. They use the railing of the broadwalk as their main passageway, leaving the scent of their urine as they go. Observe them from a respectful distance and do not reach out to touch them.

Please refrain from bringing food into the aviary.


Mekong River

The Mekong Basin houses over 850 species of fish and more species continue to be discovered. It is also home to some of the world's largest freshwater animal species such as the river giants like the Mekong giant catfish, giant freshwater stingray and Siamese carp.
